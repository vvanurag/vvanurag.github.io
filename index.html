About Me

With a Bachelor's in Mechanical Engineering followed by 2 Masters', I have a multi-disciplinary involvement with Robotics. Starting with multibody-dynamics analysis, planning and control of space manipulators, my areas of interest evolved from dymanics and control to simultaneous localization and mapping (SLAM), 3D reconstruction and is currently delving into perception and autonomy. I also strive to apply deep learning in solving each of the aforementioned problems. Please check my projects below and leave me a note at sdf if they interest you.

I bear strong foundation in geometry and linear algebra as basis of my understanding and demystifying these fields. I am also excited to completely phathom the scope of learning paradigms
