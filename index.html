About Me

Robotics Master's student

I am a second-year masters student in Robotics at the University of Maryland, College Park. I completed my undergraduation from Gujarat Technological University majoring in Electronics and Communication Engineering. My research interest lies in the areas of bio-inspired active perception, 3D computer vision, robot localization and mapping problems.

Currently, I am joint member of Perception and Robotics Group (PRG) and Perception and Autonomous Robotics Group (PeAR), advised by Prof. Yiannis Aloimonos and Prof. Nitin J. Sanket. Prior to pursing my Masters, I was Research Associate at Swaayatt Robots working on integration of visual and lidar odometry.

In my spare time I like to travel, paint and read psychology books!
